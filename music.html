KTER - Music

Permitted noise since 1900 and typing error

I am a keen musician, classically taught the clarinet and now enjoying some guitar lessons to try and help break the bad habits I taught myself many moons ago. I play with the Sevenoaks and Tonbridge concert band as well as Invicta Wind Orcestra. I am also part of the adult volunteers at 17th Tonbridge Scouts and Guides Marching Band.

I have also recently started doing some very basic production level work. I use a combination of Garageband, an iPad and an IK Multimedia iRig HD 2. I don't know if this classes as a recommendation or a "please be gentle". When inspired I sometimes add a music video but several remain as just the audio.
